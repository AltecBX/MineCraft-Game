Thomas & the Block Quest

Thomas wakes in a strange block world. Mine, build, craft, tame cats, survive the night, and fight through the fire and end dimensions to the black dragon.

Desktop. WASD or arrow keys to move, click to look, Space jump, Shift sprint, C crouch, F dodge, V camera, left click mine or attack, right click place, E use or sleep in a bed, G command a cat, J quest journal, 1 to 9 hotbar, I inventory, K skills, Esc pause. A and D strafe by default, or set them to turn in Settings. Mobile. Use the on screen controls.

Wake Up, Thomas

Thomas wakes by a cold, broken campfire deep in a giant block forest. A purple corruption is creeping through the land, and only the cats can sense it.

A supply chest waits by the fire. Find wood, craft a tool, make a friend, and uncover the first secret before night falls.
